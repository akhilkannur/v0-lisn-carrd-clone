# Cannonball GTM Framework Applied to LISN Agency UGC Service

## Core Principles

### 1. Disqualification vs. Qualification Framework
- **Tier 1 (Binary)**: Must be Indian D2C brand with active social commerce
- **Tier 2 (Bonus)**: Operating in target verticals (skincare, fashion, food, etc.)
- **Tier 3 (Validation)**: Demonstrates innovation appetite (trying new ad formats, discussing creative challenges)

### 2. Recursive Validation Architecture
- If a brand looks like a fit but the decision-maker isn't responsive to UGC messaging, adjust parameters
- If certain verticals consistently respond poorly, refine targeting
- Failed prospects feed back into discovery with adjusted criteria

### 3. Behavioral Qualification Over Demographics
- Look for brands whose marketing leaders discuss ad performance challenges
- Identify companies experiencing ad fatigue or declining ROAS
- Find businesses that have recently discussed creative challenges

## ICP Discovery Process

### Company Criteria (Hard Disqualification)
- **Must be**: Indian D2C brand with active social commerce
- **Must spend**: ₹50,000+ monthly on Facebook/Instagram ads
- **Must operate in**: Target verticals (skincare, fashion, food, etc.)
- **Must show**: Evidence of ad fatigue (decreasing engagement, changing creative)

### Person Criteria (Behavioral Validation)
- **Title**: Marketing Head, Growth Manager, Brand Manager, or Founder
- **Behavior**: Publicly discusses ad performance challenges
- **Signals**: Posts about creative fatigue, declining CTRs, or banner blindness
- **Activity**: Shares content about ad optimization struggles

### Discovery Process
1. Find companies running Facebook/Instagram ads in your verticals
2. Identify decision-makers who discuss ad challenges online
3. Validate they're experiencing the problems you solve
4. Connect if both company/person criteria met

## Tool Stack (3-Tool Limit)
1. **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** - Find individuals matching behavioral criteria
2. **Facebook Ad Library** - Identify companies with active ad spend
3. **LinkedIn/X monitoring** - Validate behavioral signals of innovation/appetite

## Approach Method
- **LinkedIn outreach** to validated prospects
- **Value-first** conversations about their ad challenges
- **Case study** sharing from similar brands
- **Demo** of authentic UGC performance vs. studio ads

## Success Metrics
- Response rate by vertical
- Conversion from first touch to consultation
- Which behavioral signals predict highest quality leads
- ROI differential between engaged vs. non-engaged prospects

## Key Insight
Focus on finding people who are already feeling the pain, not educating cold prospects.
